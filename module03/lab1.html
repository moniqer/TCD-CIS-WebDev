The History of Video Games

Exploring the evolution and impact of video games on society.

Early Beginnings

The history of video games began in the 1950s, with simple games like Tennis for Two being created for entertainment.

Golden Age of Arcade Games

During the 1970s and 1980s, arcade games like Pong and Space Invaders became incredibly popular.

Modern Video Games

In the 2000s and beyond, video games have become more immersive, with titles like Grand Theft Auto V and The Legend of Zelda pushing the boundaries of technology and storytelling.

Fun Facts about Video Games

  • The first video game ever created was Tennis for Two.
  • The video game industry is worth over $150 billion worldwide.
  • Over 2.5 billion people play video games globally.
